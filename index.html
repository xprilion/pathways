Fun time!

And the fun begins!

We asked our teachers to pick up a topic and to tell their funniest moments associated with it! Here's a summary:

Piyush Sir: (peaking on Current/Electricity) The only times I've experienced a current is when madam touches me!
(Now that's smartly done sir! Indeed!)

Shuaib Sir (the soon-to-be bridegroom!): (speaking on Exam hall) During the exam, cheating slips passing all over and around, gave me high BP!
(behti ganga mein hath dho lete sir!)

Akhilesh Sir: (speaking on Dogs) Returning home on bike, the dogs on the way would chase me. Pestered thoroughly, one day I took my car and let them chase me to their heart's fill!
(we get the idea! Time to buy a car!)

Shashi Sir: (speaking on Bunking) We went to school everyday for 2 months without attending class. We would hide and sleep!
(inspiration in its purest form! *eyes wet*)
